Where do I start?

The Transformers comics may seem intimidating at first, with over 100 comics. We can simplify this into two phases, phases 1 is more of an origin story. That includes character-spin-offs and little character build up. Phase two can be described as a soft reboot, meaning it maintains the same story arc as phase 1 but doesn't require the reader to have read the previous comics.
