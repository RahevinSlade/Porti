This is the Good Stuff

Phase 2, marks are whole new era of the Transformers. It continues after Phase 1 but, in it's own way. It doesn't require the reader to have read the previous comics but it maintain the same character build up and further explores each character development through out the series. In phase 2 you will notice not only the art style but the story itself improves tremendously, enjoy.
